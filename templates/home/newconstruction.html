Our Design Build Process

Precision construction without shortcuts

Initial Consultation & Feasibility

We review your goals, budget, and property conditions to confirm what is legally and structurally feasible under local zoning and building regulations.

{# 01#}
  • Evaluate zoning, setbacks, and lot restrictions
  • Review budget range and project timeline
  • Identify potential constraints before design begins

Design Development

We prepare floor plans, layouts, and scope of work that align with your needs, California building codes, and city requirements.

{# 02#}
  • Develop functional layouts and design concepts
  • Define project scope, materials, and finishes
  • Ensure design aligns with local code requirements

Engineering & Documentation

Structural calculations, energy compliance (Title 24), and technical drawings are prepared as required by California building departments.

{# 03#}
  • Prepare structural and foundation engineering
  • Complete Title 24 energy compliance documentation
  • Assemble full permit-ready construction drawings

Permit Submission & Approval

Permit applications are submitted to the city or county. We coordinate plan check responses and revisions until approvals are issued.

{# 04#}
  • Submit plans to city or county building departments
  • Address plan check comments and required revisions
  • Obtain permit approvals before construction starts

Construction & Inspections

Construction begins after permit approval. All required city inspections are scheduled and completed at each phase of the build.

{# 05#}
  • Perform construction according to approved plans
  • Schedule and pass all required city inspections
  • Maintain quality control and jobsite oversight

Final Approval & Project Completion

After final inspection approval, the project is completed and turned over, fully compliant with California codes and permits.

{# 06#}
  • Complete final inspection and sign-off
  • Address any remaining punch-list items
  • Deliver a fully permitted and compliant project
