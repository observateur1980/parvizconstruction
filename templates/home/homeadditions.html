Our Home Addition Process

Planning first, permits handled correctly, and construction done to code

Consultation & Feasibility

We evaluate your home and lot conditions to confirm what’s possible under zoning, setbacks, and structural limitations.

  • Evaluate zoning, lot coverage, and setbacks
  • Review goals, budget, and timeline
  • Identify structural and utility constraints

Design & Layout Planning

We develop the layout and scope of work to match your needs and ensure the addition blends with your existing home.

  • Develop floor plan and exterior integration
  • Define finishes and project scope
  • Confirm construction approach

Engineering & Permit Documents

We prepare structural calculations and permit-ready drawings for submission to your city or county.

  • Structural engineering and framing plan
  • Title 24 / energy compliance (if required)
  • Complete permit-ready plans

Permit Submission & Approval

We submit plans and handle plan check comments until permits are approved and ready for construction.

  • Submit plans to building department
  • Respond to plan check comments
  • Obtain permits and approvals

Construction & Inspections

We build the addition according to approved plans and schedule inspections at each required stage.

  • Foundation, framing, roofing, windows
  • Electrical, plumbing, HVAC integration
  • City inspections and quality control

Finishing & Final Approval

We complete finish work and obtain final inspection approval for a fully permitted and compliant addition.

  • Drywall, paint, flooring, trim
  • Final fixtures and punch-list
  • Final inspection and sign-off
