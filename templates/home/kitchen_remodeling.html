Our Kitchen Remodeling Process

Clean workflow, clear scope, and quality craftsmanship

In-Home Consultation & Measurements

We review your goals, take measurements, and understand how you use your kitchen so we can design a layout that makes sense.

  • Walkthrough + measurements
  • Discuss budget range and priorities
  • Identify any structural or utility constraints

Design, Layout & Material Selection

We finalize the layout and help you choose finishes that match your style and budget — cabinets, countertops, backsplash, flooring, and more.

  • Optimize layout (workflow, storage, lighting)
  • Choose cabinets, counters, backsplash, and fixtures
  • Confirm scope of work and timeline

Permits & Prep (If Required)

If your remodel involves electrical, plumbing, structural changes, or relocation of appliances, we handle permits and prepare the jobsite properly.

  • Permit documentation and submission (when needed)
  • Utility planning (electrical/plumbing upgrades)
  • Site protection and staging

Demolition & Rough Work

We remove existing materials and complete rough plumbing, electrical, and framing as required — all code compliant and ready for inspection.

  • Demo and disposal
  • Rough electrical and plumbing
  • Framing adjustments + inspections (if required)

Install: Cabinets, Counters, Tile & Flooring

This is where the kitchen takes shape — precise installation and finishing work for a clean, high-end result.

  • Cabinet installation and leveling
  • Countertops, backsplash, and tile
  • Flooring + paint and trim details

Final Touches & Completion

We complete final connections, install fixtures and appliances, and finish with a detailed punch-list so everything is clean and complete.

  • Install lighting, faucet, sink, appliances
  • Final inspection (if required)
  • Detailed punch-list + clean handover
