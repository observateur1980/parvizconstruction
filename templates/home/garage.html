Our Garage Remodeling Process

Organized work, clean finishes, and a garage that looks like part of the home

Consultation & Goal Planning

We evaluate your garage and discuss your goals — storage, workshop, clean parking space, home gym, or future conversion planning.

  • On-site walkthrough + measurements
  • Review current condition (walls, floor, electrical)
  • Define scope, budget, and timeline

Design Options & Material Selection

We plan the layout and select finishes — flooring system, wall finish, ceiling, lighting, storage cabinets, slatwall, and more.

  • Storage layout + parking clearance
  • Choose epoxy/polyaspartic floor options
  • Cabinets, shelves, slatwall, and lighting plan

Permits & Prep (If Required)

If the project includes electrical upgrades, new circuits, structural work, or garage door changes, we handle permits and prep the jobsite.

  • Permit documentation when needed
  • Electrical planning (EV outlet, subpanel, etc.)
  • Jobsite protection + staging

Demolition & Rough Work

We remove old storage, repair surfaces, and complete rough electrical/framing work as needed before finishing.

  • Remove old shelving/cabinets if needed
  • Wall repairs, framing, and insulation (optional)
  • Rough electrical + inspection (if required)

Finish Work: Floors, Walls, Lighting & Storage

We finish the garage with clean, durable materials — flooring, paint, lighting, cabinets, and wall systems.

  • Epoxy/polyaspartic floor coating
  • Drywall/paint and trim details
  • Install cabinets, slatwall, shelving

Final Walkthrough & Completion

We complete punch-list items, clean the space, and confirm everything is working properly — including outlets, lights, and door operation.

  • Final inspection (if required)
  • Punch-list + detail work
  • Clean handover + walkthrough
