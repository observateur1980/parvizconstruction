Our Home Remodeling Process

Clear scope, clean work, and a finished home that feels brand new

Consultation & Scope Planning

We walk the home with you and define what you want to improve — layout, finishes, repairs, and upgrades.

  • On-site walkthrough + measurements
  • Discuss priorities, budget, and timeline
  • Identify structural or utility constraints

Design, Layout & Finish Selection

We finalize the plan and help choose materials — flooring, paint, cabinets, tile, doors, lighting, and fixtures.

  • Layout optimization and flow improvement
  • Finish selections and material planning
  • Confirm scope and schedule

Permits & Documentation (If Required)

If we modify walls, utilities, or structural elements, we handle plans and permits to keep everything legal and compliant.

  • Permit drawings and submissions (if needed)
  • Title 24 / code compliance when applicable
  • Plan check responses and approvals

Demolition & Rough Construction

We protect your home, complete demolition, then handle rough framing, plumbing, and electrical as required.

  • Demo, disposal, and site protection
  • Rough framing, plumbing, and electrical
  • Inspections (if required)

Finish Work & Installation

This is where the remodel comes together — drywall, paint, flooring, cabinetry, tile, trim, and fixtures.

  • Drywall, texture, paint
  • Flooring, tile, cabinets, trim
  • Lighting and fixture installation

Final Walkthrough & Completion

We complete punch-list items, clean up, and do a final walkthrough to confirm everything is finished correctly.

  • Final inspection (if required)
  • Punch-list completion
  • Clean handover + walkthrough
