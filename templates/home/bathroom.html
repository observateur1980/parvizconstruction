Our Bathroom Remodeling Process

Waterproofing done right, clean tile work, and a finished space you’ll enjoy daily

In-Home Consultation & Measurements

We review your bathroom, take measurements, and identify what’s possible based on layout, plumbing locations, and your priorities.

  • Walkthrough + measurements
  • Discuss style, budget, and priorities
  • Identify layout or plumbing constraints

Design & Material Selection

We finalize the plan and help you choose finishes that match your goals — tile, vanity, fixtures, lighting, and shower system.

  • Confirm layout and shower design
  • Select tile, vanity, fixtures, and lighting
  • Finalize scope of work and timeline

Permits & Preparation (If Required)

If the project includes plumbing relocation, electrical changes, or structural work, we handle permits and prepare the jobsite properly.

  • Permit documentation when needed
  • Utility planning (plumbing/electrical)
  • Protection and staging

Demolition & Rough Work

We remove old materials and complete rough plumbing, electrical, and framing as required — ready for inspection if needed.

  • Demo and disposal
  • Rough plumbing & electrical
  • Framing and inspections (if required)

Waterproofing, Tile & Install

We install a proper waterproofing system, then complete tile work, vanity installation, and fixture setup for a clean, durable finish.

  • Shower waterproofing system
  • Tile walls & floors
  • Vanity, glass, fixtures installation

Final Touches & Completion

We complete final connections, detail work, and punch-list items. If required, we schedule final inspections and sign-off.

  • Final connections + details
  • Final inspection (if required)
  • Clean handover + walkthrough
